Recovery Whooper

Client Hungry Jacks (Speculative)

Through Burger King may be known differently down south, the Whopper still retains its iconic flame grilled goodness. But in a country ravaged by the very thing that gives the burger its iconic flavour, how could Hungry Jack’s give back to the community?

Introducing the Recovery Whopper, a special edition made to help rebuild Australia one burger at a time. Flame grilled with charcoal made from the burnt forest trees, proceeds are donated to help in the recovery of lost homes, communities and greenery.
