Pong the Chatbot

Client GrabFood Art Director Scott Miao
Agency 72andSunny Singapore Junior Writer Ang Peng Da
Creative Lead Isaac Tan

With Indonesia becoming one of Southeast Asia’s largest ecommerce markets, it has become quintessential for companies to offer customer services around the clock in the digital space. And this is no exception for GrabFood, who came to us to develop a more memorable experience for their chatbot on Line.

We turned the experience into a small game where users could level up their grain-of-rice-buddy, Pong. From finding food deals to recommended restaurants, each interaction helped Pong level up with fancier outfits.
