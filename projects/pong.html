Pong the Chatbot

Indonesia is becoming one of Southeast Asia’s largest ecommerce markets. And it has become quintessential for companies to offer customers services around the clock at the click of a button. And this is no exception for GrabFood, who came to us to develop a more memorable experience for their chatbot users on LINE.

We turned experience into a mini-game for users. Featuring the character Pong, users could evolve and alter its appearance with every action they make. This took advantage of LINE’s sticker system, allowing for illustrations for new features and emotions to be easily added.
