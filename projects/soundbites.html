Soundbites

Southeast Asia is the foodies’ heaven. With thousands upon thousands of dishes, many young and hungry millennials are turning to Instagram to find inspiration for their next meal.

But in this extremely crowded environment of food porn, it’s hard for any brand to post a beautiful food snap and hope to stand out. To help GrabFood cut through the clutter, we leveraged on the platform’s Insta-story feature to captivate users with sound.

With the help of a rather eccentric GrabFood Foley Artist, we recrated the sounds of a dish being made, followed by showcasing featured GrabFood merchants. The result was a framework that GrabFood could easily replicate across the variety of meals across the region, starting with the Phillippines. Videos were shot in both vertical and horizontal formats, to allow for further flexibility in making YouTube bumpers and Facebook ads.
