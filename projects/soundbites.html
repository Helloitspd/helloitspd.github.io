Soundbites

Client GrabFood Art Director Scott Miao
Agency 72andSunny Singapore Junior Writer Ang Peng Da
Creative Lead Isaac Tan Film Production Little Red Ants

Although millions of young millennials turn to Instagram for food inspiration, many companies, bloggers and users saturate the platform with tasty images of foodporn. GrabFood wanted to create a new content series that would cut across the clutter and stand out in the crowded landscape. With the help of a rather eccentric GrabFood Foley artist, we created Soundbites — an Instastory content series that whet appetites through sound.

We launched our pilot series in the Philippines, developing a framework that GrabFood could then take and adapt across different markets.
