Soundbites

Without a doubt, Southeast Asia is the foodies’ heaven. With thousands upon thousands of dishes, many young and hungry millennials are turning to Instagram to find inspiration for their next meal.

But in this extremely crowded environment of food porn, it’s impossible for any brand to stand out with just a beautiful snap of their dishes. To help GrabFood cut through the clutter, we leveraged on the platform’s Insta-story feature and captivate users with sound.

Through a rather eccentric GrabFood Foley Artist, we recreated the sounds of a popular dish being made, followed by showcasing featured GrabFood merchants. The result was a framework that GrabFood could then easily replicate across the many dishes across the region, starting with the Phillippines. Videos were shot in both vertical and horizontal formats, allowing for further flexibility in making YouTube bumpers and Facebook ads.
