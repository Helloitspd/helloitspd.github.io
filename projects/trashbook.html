Trashbook

School Glasgow School of Art (Singapore)

For one of our school's exhibitions, we were tasked to create a book that best represented our first 100 days. I wanted to represent our learnings of being more bold and experimental with our work by looking for a different process in making a book. This led to experiments with different materials collected from bins and friends, and a very upset laser printer.

From plastic bags to receipts, this experiment of printing on trash led to a narrative that poked fun at today's throw-away consumerist culture.
