Diabetes: A Living Hell

School Glasgow School of Art (Singapore)

Singapore has been on war against diabetes for many years. But despite the many efforts to curb the disease, the number of diabetics remains on the rise. Many campaigns have tried to tackle the issues with messaging meant for everyone, young and old. As a result, many younger Singaporeans tend to think that they would only need to be concerned in their later years.

However, Type 2 diabetes is a result of the lifestyle choices a person makes since young. I felt that there needed to be a harder hitting campaign to drive the message home for the younger generation. Inspired by how religion plays an important role in nurturing values, the campaign juxtaposed the horrors and suffering of hell against the disease.
