Unlimited Posters

Client UOB Junior Art Director Wynn Lim
Agency 72andSunny Singapore Junior Writer Ang Peng Da
Creative Lead Zara Kok

For many businesses across different industries, finding the right person for certain jobs can prove to be quite a challenge. However, many businesses including UOB found the solution after reaching out to a group of candidates forgotten by many employers — people with disabilities.

Having found success in solving their manpower issues, UOB launched The Unlimited, an initiative set out to help make it easier for companies to hire people with disabilities. We created a set of gift posters inspired by the real achievements that people with disabilities have made with UOB partners.
