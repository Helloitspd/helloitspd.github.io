Unlimited Posters

For some businesses, filling in certain critical positions can be challenging. And sometimes, it’s not about finding the person with the right qualifications, but someone with the right attitude.

At UOB, they met their staffing challenge at the UOB ScanHub, a facility that digitises millions documents a year. Faced with a high turnover rate, they found their solution when they reached out to a group of forgotten candidates — people with disabilities.

Wanting to share their success and encourage other businesses to think differently, UOB launched a campaign targeted at CEOs of businesses, big and small. We created a set of gift posters to accompany the campaign. These posters were inspired by the real achievements accomplished by people with disabilities.
